Answers

Everything you need to know before partnering with us.

How do engagements typically start?+
We begin with a 90-minute working session to map your revenue engine, cash posture, and immediate decisions. From there, we propose a 90-day plan with clear milestones.
Will you work directly with my leadership team?+
Yes. We join your exec meeting cadence, partner with Sales/RevOps on pipeline health, and embed with your controller or accounting firm to keep books clean.
Do you replace or complement an in-house CFO?+
We often stand up finance before a full-time hire. We also coach in-house finance leaders with benchmarks, systems, and playbooks so you get leverage immediately.
What industries do you specialize in?+
We primarily support B2B SaaS, marketplaces, and services companies between Seed and Series C. We have dedicated operators for each model.
How quickly can you start?+
Discovery and onboarding typically take two weeks. We prioritize quick wins like scorecards and cash clarity within the first month.
